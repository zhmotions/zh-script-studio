ZH Script Studio v2.0

Quick start

  1. ＋ New — open a .docx, .txt or .srt script. It auto-reloads next time.
  2. Select text in the script.
  3. Send it to your timeline with the buttons below.

Timeline buttons

  • ＋ Title — inserts the selected text as one editable, animated title at the playhead.
  • ≣ Batch — every selected line becomes its own title, placed one after another (each lasts the Dur seconds).
  • ⚑ Marker — drops a timeline marker at the playhead, with the selected text as the marker comment. No graphic — for notes/navigation.
  • ⧉ Copy — copies the selection to the clipboard.
  • Dur — seconds each title stays on screen.

🎬 Template (animation)

Titles use the bundled ZH Default animation. Click 🎬 Template to add your own .mogrt (made in After Effects with Source Text added to Essential Graphics). Added templates are saved permanently as chips — click a chip to switch, ✕ to remove. No re-uploading.

🎨 Mark text

Select text in the script, then click a colour to colour it, the yellow swatch to highlight, or the last swatch to clear.

⚙ Display

Font, size, line spacing, text/background colour (swatches) and zoom. These apply in reading mode (turn off Preserve document styling to adjust freely).

Works in

Adobe Premiere Pro & After Effects (25.0 and newer).
